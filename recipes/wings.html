Chicken Wings

Spicy, flavorful fried food native to Buffalo, NY

Ingredients

  • Chicken Wings
  • Hot Sauce
  • Butter
  • Blue Cheese

Steps

  1. Set fryer to 350
  2. Drop wings in fryer, cook 8 minutes
  3. Microwave butter until melted
  4. Combine hot sauce with butter, drench wings
  5. Enjoy!
