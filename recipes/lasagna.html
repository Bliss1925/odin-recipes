Lasagna

A tasty, hearty Italian dish made with pasta, sauce, cheese and beef.

Ingrdients

  • Tomato sauce
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Mozzerella, Ricotta and Parmesan cheese
  • Ground Beef

Steps

  1. Boil and cook lasagna Noodles
  2. Brown ground beef
  3. Layer beef, sauce and ricotta cheese in pan, about 5 layers
  4. Top with mozzerella and parmesan
  5. Bake at 400 degrees for 30 minutes
